About Me

Welcome to my personal blog! I’m Yuhang Wu, a student at the University of Manchester with a deep passion for artificial intelligence, specifically in the areas of Retrieval Augmented Generation (RAG) and Large Language Models (LLMs). This blog serves as a platform for me to document my learning journey, share my research, and discuss various projects I’m working on.

My interest in AI is driven by a curiosity to explore how technology can be harnessed to solve complex problems, especially those involving large datasets and intricate reasoning tasks. Through this blog, I aim to share insights, study notes, and project updates, hoping they might be helpful or inspiring to others who share similar interests.

I value continuous learning and believe in the power of community and collaboration. While I may not have all the answers, I’m always eager to engage in discussions, exchange ideas, and learn from others.

Feel free to explore my posts and projects, and if something resonates with you, I’d love to hear your thoughts.
